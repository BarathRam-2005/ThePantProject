Interesting Facts About our Formal Trousers

               
                   
                       
                           
Historical Significance
                           

Formal trousers, as we know them today, became popular in the early 19th century when knee-length breeches                                 were gradually replaced by full-length trousers, influenced by British and French fashion.

                       
                   
                   
                       
                           
World Record
                           

The longest trousers ever made measured 65.5 feet (20 meters) long                                 and were created in Peru as part of a Guinness World Record attempt.

                       
                   
                   
                       
                           
Modern Style
                           

With advancements in tailoring, formal trousers saw variations in fit, from the wide-legged 1920s                                 style to the slim-cut designs of the 1960s and the fitted, tapered styles popular today.
